Who we are

We believe in a world where you have total freedom to be you, without judgement. To experiment, to express yourself, to be brave and grab life as the extraordinary adventure it is. So we make sure everyone has an equal chance to discover all the amazing things they are capable of no matter who they are, where they are from or what looks like a boss to them.We exist to give you tge confidence to be whoever you want to be.

Fashion democracy

Choice for all

Our audience (AKA you) is wonderfully unique, and we do everyting we can to help you finf your fit. We offer our Fleur Brands in more than 30 sizes and we are committed to providing all sizes at the same prices - so you can be confident we've got the perfect thing for you.

Body positivity

It's important for us to promote body image - we are not about conforming to any stereotypes - so we work with more than 200 models to represent our audience. And we are not in the business of digitally altering their appearnce either there's no reshaping or removing stretch marks here. Our models are part of the Fleur family and we support them by following a Model Welfare Policy
